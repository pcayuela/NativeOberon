Another Oberon
 
Oberon is the outermost of Uranus's
	known satellites and second largest: 
	 distance from Uranus: 583,420 km 
	 diameter: 1523 km 
	 mass: 3.03e21 kg 
 Pronounced "OH buh ron". 
 Oberon is the King of the Fairies and
 husband of Titania in Shakespeare's
 Midsummer-Night's Dream.
